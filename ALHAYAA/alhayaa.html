What is Alhayaa Boutique?

Alhayaa Boutique is an online store dedicated to modest fashion, offering a refined selection of abayas and hijabs that blend elegance with simplicity.

You can order by sending us a direct message on Instagram with the item name, color, and size. Our team will confirm your order and guide you through payment and delivery.
We reply to messages every day until 8 PM. Any messages received after that will be answered the next morning, in shaa Allah.

Yes, we offer delivery across Algeria 58 wilayas. Delivery fees vary by location and will be shared with you when confirming your order.
